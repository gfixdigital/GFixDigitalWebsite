Founder & CEO

GFix Digital – The Digital Hub

Muhammad Abbas

Founder & CEO | Digital Ecosystem Builder

Muhammad Abbas is the Founder and CEO of GFix Digital, a growing digital solutions platform focused on bridging the gap between businesses and skilled digital talent.

He is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science while serving as an Administrator and IT Trainer at Modern Educational Proficiency Academy (MEPA). He is also actively working as a Digital Skills Trainer at the BanoQabil IT Program, one of the largest IT training initiatives in Pakistan.

With practical experience in graphic designing, video editing, and digital marketing, he leads GFix Digital with a clear vision to build a complete digital ecosystem where services, skills, and opportunities are connected.

Beyond technical expertise, he is deeply committed to youth development through soft skills — including leadership, communication, teamwork, presentation skills, interview preparation, collaboration, and critical thinking — believing these are essential for long-term success in the modern world.

He has also conducted multiple training sessions and workshops in schools, colleges, and institutions, helping students understand digital skills, freelancing opportunities, and career pathways.

Under his leadership, GFix Digital is evolving into a platform where learning, working, and growth happen together — enabling both businesses and individuals to move forward in the digital space.
